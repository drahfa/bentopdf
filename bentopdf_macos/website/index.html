❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All PDF processing happens locally on your Mac. Your documents are never uploaded to any server.

Does SitiPDF require an internet connection?

No. SitiPDF works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection.

Is there a subscription?

No subscription required. SitiPDF is a one-time purchase with free updates.

What macOS versions are supported?

SitiPDF supports macOS 12 (Monterey) and later, optimized for both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
