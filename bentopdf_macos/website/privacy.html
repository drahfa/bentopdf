SitiPDF does not collect any data. Your documents never leave your Mac. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking. Period.

Overview

SitiPDF ("we", "our", or "the app") is a native macOS application developed by VSG Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use SitiPDF.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

SitiPDF is designed from the ground up to be privacy-first:

  • No user accounts required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No telemetry data sent to external servers
  • No advertising or marketing data collection
  • No crash reports sent without your consent

Document Processing

All PDF processing happens locally on your Mac.

When you use SitiPDF to edit, merge, split, annotate, or convert PDFs:

  • Your documents are processed entirely on your device
  • No files are uploaded to any server
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • Your documents remain under your complete control

Third-Party Services

SitiPDF does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service involved is the Mac App Store for distribution and updates, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Data Storage

SitiPDF may store the following data locally on your Mac:

  • Application preferences (theme, language settings)
  • Recent file history (optional, stored locally)

This data is stored in your Mac's standard application data directories and is never transmitted externally.

Children's Privacy

SitiPDF does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we don't collect any data, we have no way to notify you directly of changes.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. You have complete control over any files you create or edit with SitiPDF.
